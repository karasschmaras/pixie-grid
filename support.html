Frequently asked

Are puzzles always solvable without guessing?

Yes. Every puzzle has exactly one solution and can be solved by pure logic — never by guessing.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Settings → Restore Purchases in the app. This asks Apple to re-confirm your previous purchases (e.g. Remove Ads) — no account needed.

Are there ads, timers, or lives?

No forced ads, no timers, and no lives. Optional rewarded videos only ever grant a small help (a hint or a revealed cell) — never required. Every puzzle is fully solvable for free.

Is my data collected?

No. Pixie Grid is fully offline and collects no personal data — see our Privacy Policy.

I missed a daily — did I lose my streak?

No punishment. You can solve a missed daily later; streaks are gentle by design.
