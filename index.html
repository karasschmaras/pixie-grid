Pixie Grid

A cozy picture-reveal nonogram puzzle.

Solve the grid, reveal the picture. Play a relaxing campaign across five difficulty tiers, earn mastery stars, take on fresh daily puzzles plus daily & weekly quests, and unlock collectible background skins.

Free to play, with ads you can remove anytime. No energy gate, no lives, no pay-to-win, no loot boxes — every level is solvable for free. No forced timer on the board; an optional timed mode is there if you want it (off by default).
