Pixie Grid — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

Pixie Grid does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any of your personal data. The game runs entirely on your device, works fully offline, and has no servers, no accounts, no ad-tracking, and no analytics that ever leave your iPhone. That is the whole policy — the rest is detail.

1. Who we are

Pixie Grid is made by Karas Games (“we”, “us”). Questions about this policy: karasleon@web.de.

2. What data we collect

None. We do not collect any personal data. Specifically, Pixie Grid:

  • has no user accounts and no login — we never ask for your name, email, or any identifier;
  • makes no network connections — fully offline, with no backend server to send anything to;
  • contains no third-party SDKs — no ad networks, analytics, attribution, or crash-reporting services;
  • does not access your location, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or health data;
  • does not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and does not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Data that stays on your device

Pixie Grid saves your progress — solved puzzles, your collection and room, settings, streak, and help tokens — locally on your device only, using Apple’s on-device storage. This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is not synced to iCloud. You can erase all of it at any time by deleting the app. A small amount of on-device-only usage information (e.g. that a daily puzzle was completed) stays in the app on your device, is never transmitted, contains no personal information, and never includes any puzzle answer.

4. In-app purchases

Pixie Grid offers optional in-app purchases (a one-time “Remove Ads” purchase and cosmetic packs). All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store / StoreKit. We never see your payment details or any information that identifies you. Use the in-app Restore Purchases control to ask Apple — not us — to re-confirm your entitlements.

5. Advertising

Pixie Grid contains no banner ads, no interstitial ads, and no forced ads. No advertising network is active in this version, and we do not track you for advertising. If a future version ever introduces tracking, we will update this policy, update our App Store privacy labels, and show Apple’s App Tracking Transparency prompt first — and you can always decline.

6. Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable). No behavioral ads, no third-party tracking, no advertising identifier, no accounts. This complies with COPPA and equivalent children’s-privacy rules by collecting no data at all.

7. Your rights (GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA)

Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, delete, sell, or share. We do not sell or share your personal information. Questions: karasleon@web.de. The only data the app holds is local game state on your device, fully under your control and removed when you delete the app.

8. International data transfers

Because no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international transfers of your personal data.

9. Data retention

We retain no personal data, because we collect none. Local game state remains on your device until you delete it or the app.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles data, we will update this policy, change the “Last updated” date, and update the app’s App Store privacy labels before that version ships.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your privacy: karasleon@web.de (Karas Games).
